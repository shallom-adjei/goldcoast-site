FAQ

Frequently Asked

The minimum investment is ₵200. You can invest any amount above this threshold.
You can deposit via MTN Mobile Money or cryptocurrency (USDT, BTC, ETH). Simply choose your preferred method in the dashboard.
Returns vary by plan: Starter gives 50% earnings (e.g. invest ₵1,000 → get ₵1,500), Growth gives 80% earnings (e.g. invest ₵1,000 → get ₵1,800), and Premium gives 100% earnings (e.g. invest ₵1,000 → get ₵2,000). All payouts include your original capital.
Returns depend on your chosen plan: Starter (7 days), Growth (14 days), or Premium (21 days). When your investment matures – a Withdraw button will appear.
