Why We’re Still Here

Most platforms disappear. We built ours to last — here’s how.

The Reinvestment Cycle

When your plan matures, you take a portion of your profit as instant cashback. Your principal stays in the system and keeps growing. This means we never pay out more than we hold, and your money always has a job to do. Platforms that let everyone withdraw 100% at once always collapse. We don’t.

Active Plan Protection

To withdraw your full balance, you need an active investment plan of at least the same size as your original investment. This ensures that large withdrawals are always backed by fresh capital. It’s the same principle banks use — and it’s why your funds are always available when you need them.

Instant Cashback Rewards

Every time your plan matures, you get real cash instantly — not just numbers on a screen. Meanwhile, the rest of your funds are already working in your next plan. You earn, you withdraw, and your principal keeps building. It’s the smartest way to grow wealth without risking the platform.

Community Power

Our members earn ₵15 per referral, and VIP members enjoy boosted bonuses, priority support, and exclusive perks. The more you share, the more you earn — and a growing community keeps the platform strong for everyone.

This is why we’ve paid out millions and kept growing — while others shut down. Your money is safe, your rewards are real, and your future is bright.

Gold Coast Capital – Membership Agreement

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Welcome to the Gold Coast Capital community! By creating an account or using our platform, you agree to this Membership Agreement. It explains how the platform works and what you can expect as a valued member.

1. About Gold Coast Capital

Gold Coast Capital is a digital wealth‑building platform that helps you invest consistently and earn generous bonuses on your investments. Our mission is to make investing rewarding, transparent, and accessible.

2. Investment Plans

  • Starter – Minimum ₵200, 10‑day duration, 50% bonus
  • Growth – Minimum ₵300, 20‑day duration, 70% bonus
  • Premium – Minimum ₵500, 30‑day duration, 100% bonus

The bonus is paid at maturity together with your original deposit. You can have multiple active plans at once.

3. Instant Cashback (Profit Share)

When your plan matures, you can immediately withdraw 20% of your profit as instant cashback — sent directly to your wallet. Your original deposit and the remaining profit stay in your account and can be unlocked by starting a new investment plan of at least the same deposit amount.

4. Withdrawal Policy

To withdraw the full balance of a matured plan, you need an active investment plan with a deposit amount at least equal to the original investment. This ensures the platform remains sustainable and rewards consistent investors.

5. Referral Rewards

Every member receives a unique referral code. When a new user signs up with your code and activates their first investment plan, you receive a cash bonus. There is no limit on referrals.

6. Prizes & Rewards

Our Telegram group runs regular events like the Daily Lucky Spin and Mystery Box. Winnings go to your Prizes & Rewards balance and can be withdrawn once you meet the minimum amount and have an active investment plan.

7. Reinvest & Earn

You can reinvest your matured payout into a new plan and receive an immediate bonus. Choose the same plan (Tier 1) or switch to a different plan with an extra deposit (Tier 2) for an even larger bonus.

8. Platform Sustainability

Our policies are designed to keep the platform stable and growing. By requiring active plans for large withdrawals and rewarding consistent investments, we’ve built a system that can serve members for years — unlike platforms that collapse when too many users withdraw at once.

9. General Provisions

This Agreement may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Platform after changes means you accept the new terms. If you have questions, our support team is always available.
