6. Profit Share and Locked Balance Policy

At maturity, an investor may immediately withdraw 20% of the profit as instant cashback, regardless of active plan status. The principal + remaining 80% of the profit becomes a “Locked Balance” and is not available for withdrawal until the user has an active investment (or combined active investments) with a total deposit amount at least equal to the original deposit of that matured plan.

If the user already has a qualifying active balance at the time of the first withdrawal request, the full payout (100%) is available immediately. If not, only the 20% cashback is released, and the remaining locked balance can only be accessed after the user meets the active balance requirement through new deposits or reinvestments.

Reinvested capital follows the same rules: the new investment's original deposit is the full reinvested amount. A matured plan that has been fully paid out (all withdrawals marked as “paid”) is considered consumed; its original deposit is deducted from the available active balance for subsequent unlocks, ensuring that a single active plan cannot be used repeatedly to unlock multiple matured plans beyond its total deposit value.

This policy is designed to prevent mass withdrawals, encourage reinvestment, and ensure the Platform always holds more capital than it owes, thereby guaranteeing long‑term sustainability.

7. Withdrawal Policy and Conditions

Withdrawals from matured plans are processed in three stages: Pending (request submitted), Approved (verified by admin), and Paid (funds transferred). The timeline for each stage depends on administrative processing and may be subject to delays due to high volume or security reviews.

To withdraw any amount beyond the 20% instant cashback, the user must have a total active balance (sum of all active plans) that meets or exceeds the original deposit of the matured plan. Additionally, the available active balance (total active minus amounts already consumed by previous full‑payout unlocks) must be sufficient to cover the required amount.

Referral bonuses may be withdrawn once the bonus balance reaches a minimum of ₵50, provided the user has at least one active investment plan. Game balance (Prizes & Rewards) may be withdrawn once the balance reaches a minimum of ₵30, also requiring an active investment plan. The Platform reserves the right to adjust these thresholds at any time.

All withdrawal requests are subject to administrative review. The Platform may request additional identity verification for large or suspicious withdrawals. Rejected withdrawals will be refunded to the user's bonus or game balance as applicable.

8. Reinvestment Program (Tier 1 & Tier 2)

Users may reinvest the full payout of a matured plan into a new plan through our Reinvest & Earn program.

Tier 1 (Same Plan): Reinvest the entire payout into the same plan type (e.g., Growth → Growth) with no additional deposit required. A bonus of ₵5 is credited to the user's Game Balance. Maximum two consecutive Tier 1 reinvestments per plan; after the second, a fresh deposit (Tier 2) is required.

Tier 2 (Upgrade & Boost): Choose a different plan type and make an additional fresh deposit that meets the minimum of the new plan. A bonus of 7% of the fresh deposit (capped at ₵30) is credited to the user's Game Balance. This resets the reinvestment chain and creates a new plan with the combined total (payout + fresh deposit).

Reinvestments must be initiated within 72 hours of plan maturity. After this window, the reinvestment bonus is forfeited, and the user may only withdraw according to the standard withdrawal policy. The original investment is marked as “reinvested” and cannot be withdrawn thereafter.
